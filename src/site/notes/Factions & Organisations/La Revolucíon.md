La Revolucíon aims to drive the greedy colonists from the land and return it to the people of [[Atlas/Civilisations/San Citlán\|San Citlán]].

They are allied with the [[Player Handbook/Character Affiliation Options/Freedom Fighter Fundamentals\|Freedom Fighter Fundamentals]] who are based in the Citadel.
