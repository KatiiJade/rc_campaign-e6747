Exandria exists in a [[Atlas/Planes/Material Plane\|Material Plane]] although it features connections to other planes of existence, such as the portals to the elemental planes guarded by the Ashari.

**Calendar**
An Exandrian year lasts 328 days over the course of 11 months. Each day is 24 hours in length and the 7 days of the week are named Miresen, Grissen, Whelsen, Conthsen, Folsen, Yulisen, and Da'leysen.
