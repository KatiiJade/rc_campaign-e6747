The dwarven elder is the founder of the [[Factions & Organisations/Palace of Exile\|Palace of Exile]]. He was a refugee of the war in the land of [[Atlas/Civilisations/San Citlán\|San Citlán]]. He swore that while his homeland was occupied, he would be an exile and would make his home in the Palace, alongside any others with nowhere else to go.
