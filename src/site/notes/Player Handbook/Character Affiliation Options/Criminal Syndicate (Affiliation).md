## Main Objective
Conduct criminal activities as directed by your boss.

## Assignments
This can be either a highly directive or highly autonomous group patron.

Quests for this patron can include acquisitions and retrievals, heists, gang warfare, enforcement (keep other syndicate members in line) , assassination, and topple the powerful.

## Benefits
- Gold for completed assignments.
- Access to contraband.
- Access to Criminal Syndicate's equipment and tools.
- Ability to fence items.
- Access to safe houses.
- Proficiency in one of Atheletic, Deception, Intimidation, Stealth, Slieght of Hand, or Thieves Tools.
