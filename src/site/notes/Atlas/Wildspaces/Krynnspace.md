*Source: Spelljammer Krynnspace AD&D 2e 9409*

Krynnspace is a solar system like the solar systems in the Forgotten Realms ([[Atlas/Wildspaces/Realmspace\|Realmspace]]) and World of Greyhawk ([[Atlas/Wildspaces/Greyspace\|Greyspace]]) - and in other fantasy and science fiction games. Most of the planets are named after Krynnish gods. 

Krynnspace is roughly 8,000 million miles in diameter- considerably large than [[Atlas/Wildspaces/Realmspace\|Realmspace]].

**Planets**
- Sirion
- Reorx
- [[Atlas/Worlds/Krynn\|Krynn]]
- Chislev
- Zivilyn
- Nehzmyth
- Stellar Islands
