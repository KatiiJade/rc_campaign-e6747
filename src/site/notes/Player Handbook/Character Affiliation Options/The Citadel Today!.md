Based west of the [[Preserve of Ancestors\|Preserve of Ancestors]], The Citadel Today! is the main news source of the denizens of the Citadel. Their reporters include a variety of interesting topics from the Citadel and the founding Civilisations.
The printing press utilises recent advancements in science, technology, and magic to produce visually stunning newspapers with moving images and text.
It is rumoured that the company is funding research at the [[Player Handbook/Character Affiliation Options/Radiant Academy\|Radiant Academy]] to create arcane recording devices.

### Main Objective
Investigate and report in the Citadel Newspaper on topics in the Citadel and Great Civilisations.

### Patron Type
Newspaper.

#### Newspaper Location
West of the Preserve of Ancestors.

#### Newspaper Structure
- Chief Editor - Isidora Maroto
- Editors - Nelli Ixqui
- Reporters
- Press Production

### Assignments
Characters will work as Reporters and take on a variety of assignments to gather stories to be published in the newspaper. These can be from the Citadel or Civilisations. 

There will be a job board with leads to take on. Sometimes, the Editors may request Reporters to take on a job directly. Alternatively, reporters can sell their own stories.

Reporters can choose to write articles on a range of topics, including, but not limited to:
- Interplanar Events
- Politics
- Business
- Culture
- Sports
- Science/Technology
- Magic
- Religion
- Entertainment

### Benefits
- Starting Salary of 10g per week or Gold paid for freelance stories submitted.
- Access to story leads (employees only, freelancers must source their own leads).
- Accommodation and travel allowances for off-Citadel assignments.
- Access to research materials and Calligraphers Supplies.
- Access to printing press equipment (personal use within reason).
- Press Access. Identification and papers which may secure you with access to certain people or areas.
- Proficency in one of Deception, History, Insight, Investigation, Perception, Performance, Persuasion, or Calligrapher's Tools.


### Reporter Roles
- Civilian - designated writer, political cartoonist, or chronicler of party's adventures.
- Face - High Charisma, skill suggestions Persuasion, Deception, Intimidation and Insight.
- Muscle - has a knack for getting people to talk, often former soldiers or more-or-less reformed criminals.
- Networker - extensive use of contacts and friends, High Charisma.
- Snoop -  High Intelligence, skill suggestions Investigation, Arcana, History, Religion.

### Allies
Allies, rivals, and enemies vary depending on the stories, if any, shed them in a positive or negative light.
