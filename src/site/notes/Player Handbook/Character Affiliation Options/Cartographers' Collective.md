In a beautiful house near the [[Passage of Respite\|Passage of Respite]], a loose grouping of interested geographers holds lectures and keeps a library of maps relating to all the [[Atlas/Radiant Citadel\|Radiant Citadel]] civilisations for reference. Collective members wear small brass pins showing thirteen small jewels orbiting a diamond. [[Jayvyn\|Jayvyn]] greets any guests and introduces them to the collection. [[Jayvyn\|Jayvyn]] is a scholar unmoored from his home and stranded in the Ethereal. He founded the Collective in order to help him find his lost home. There are thousands of maps at the Collective now so [[Jayvyn\|Jayvyn]] acts as a librarian.

The Collective is well-known throughout the [[Atlas/Radiant Citadel\|Radiant Citadel]] civilisations and is affiliated with different local organisations and boasts about 500 members. ## Main Objective
Discover, create, and maintain maps for the Great Civilisations; with a particular interest in rediscovering the Lost Civilisations.

## Assignments
This group patron can be either highly directive or highly autonomous.

Characters can take on a variety of quests or assignments to gather information, locating maps and relics, cartographing maps of rediscovered civilisations or dungeons, and exploration missions.

## Benefits
- Gold and rewards for completed assignments.
- Accommodation and hospitality at Allied Organisations for quests or Cartographer related adventures.
- Up to three free copies of maps per year per fellow.
- Access to map library.
- Access to various meeting and study facilities.
- Proficiency in Cartographer's Supplies. If already proficient in Cartographer Supplies, please choose one other tool or skill.
- Access to Cartographer's Supplies and additional tools.

## Fellow Cohorts
Fellow roles can include:
- Researcher - Acolyte, Entertainer, Guild Artisan, Sage
- Negotiator - Charlatan, Entertainer, Guild Artisan, Noble, Sailor
- Saboteur - Charlatan, Criminal, Guild Artisan, Soldier, Urchin
- Guard - Criminal, Folk Hero, Outlander, Sailor, Soldier
- Explorer - Acolyte, Folk Hero, Guild Artisan, Outlander, Sailor
- Expert - Any

The Cartographer's Collective is adjoined to a [[Temple of Oghma\|Temple of Oghma]] (God of Knowledge). Acolytes of [[Oghma\|Oghma]] are often also Fellows of the Collective.
